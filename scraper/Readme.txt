Scrapper parses information from the site AirBlue - https://www.airblue.com/

For testing the scraper it is recommended to use the following points of departure and destination:
to DXB from LHE
to DXB from ISB 
to ISB from JED 
to ISB from KHI (flights every day)
to ISB from SHJ
to JED from KHI 
to JED from LHE (flights every day)
to JED from MUX
to KHI from LHE (flights every day)
or vice versa

For other destinations, flights may not be available.
